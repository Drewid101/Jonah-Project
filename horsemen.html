"The beast of burden will quicken the journey", you said to yourself, "and clearly this is a divine gift!", you reasoned. You placed your load onto the camel's back and rode off thinking of how blessed you are. You had not gone much farther when you saw a glimmering expanse on the desert floor. An oasis! Surely God is blessing me in an abundant way! You dismount to camp at the water edge and refill your gourds for the ride ahead.

Your eyes grew heavy from the weary day`s travel, but no sooner did you start to drift into sleep, you heard the sound of hooves splashing through the body of water. To your horror, three figures on camel backs armed with swords, coming directly toward you. One if the figures shouted, "There it is!" the weapon pointing in the direction of your four-legged "blessing". “…and there is the dog who took it!” said another in a tone that did not sound like a voice of reason.
