Your water supply was depleted by late evening of the second day. Second thoughts of obeying the call to the mission and blaming the Divine for your current circumstances flooded your mind. In your frustration, you grumbled against the LORD Almighty and cursed the journey.

No sooner had the words fall from your lips, but warm air brushed against your face and what sounded like hundreds of thousands of thundering horse hooves. You looked behind you to see a violent sandstorm rapidly approaching. You franticly look around for any form of shelter. A rugged mountain range lies ahead of you, but some distance away. There is uncertainty of making it there in time before the storm reaches you.

Hurry to the mountain range

Cry out to the LORD
